IEEE BigData 2024 Cup: Detection of suicide risk on social media

Overview

The IEEE BigData 2024 Cup Challenges, centered on suicide ideation detection, is an important activity held annually since 2013 under the auspices of the IEEE International Conference on Big Data. This competition will span several months, culminating with the announcement of the winners at the IEEE BigData 2024 conference, scheduled for December 15-18, 2024, in Washington DC, USA.

Task Description

The task in this challenge is to design an accurate method for classifying Reddit posts into four suicide risk levels: indicator, ideation, behavior, and attempt. The available training data contains 500 instances (Reddit posts) with labeled suicide risk levels and 1500 unlabeled Reddit posts.

Paper Submission Guidelines

Important: Even if a paper performs well in the initial review, its final publication depends on the conference presentation performance and comparison with other challenge entries.

  • Format: Papers must adhere to the IEEE Computer Society Proceedings Manuscript Formatting Guidelines. View guidelines
  • Page Limit: Papers should not exceed 10 pages, including all figures, tables, and references in the IEEE 2-column format.

Selection Process

  1. Initial Review: Domain experts will conduct an initial review of the submitted papers.
  2. Conference Presentation: Papers that pass the initial review will present at the cup challenge sessions during the IEEE BigData conference.
  3. Final Selection: The Big Data Cup Chairs will evaluate all presentations. Based on the presentation performance, approximately 10 top-performing teams (across all cup challenges) will be selected to publish their papers in the conference proceedings.
